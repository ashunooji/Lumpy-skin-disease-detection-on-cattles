This Cow Has Lumpy skin Disease!!!

  • Lumpy skin disease (LSD) is a viral disease that affects cattle, causing the development of nodules or lumps on the skin.
  • It is transmitted through direct contact with infected animals or insect vectors.
  • LSD can result in reduced milk production, weight loss, and decreased fertility in cattle.

Solution

  • There's no medicine to this disease, isolate the cattle and give painkillers
  • In order to not transmit the disease to other cattles, vaccinate all the cattles
